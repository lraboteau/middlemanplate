Static site generators have become the go-to solution for building fast, secure, and scalable blogs. Let's explore why and how to build one.

## Why Static Sites?

Static sites offer numerous advantages:

READMORE

1. **Speed** - No database queries, just pure HTML
2. **Security** - No server-side vulnerabilities
3. **Scalability** - Easy to serve from CDNs
4. **Cost** - Free or cheap hosting options

## Choosing a Static Site Generator

There are many options available:

- **Middleman** - Ruby-based, flexible
- **Jekyll** - Ruby, great for blogs
- **Hugo** - Go, extremely fast
- **Eleventy** - JavaScript, simple

## Setting Up Your Blog

1. Choose your generator
2. Set up your development environment
3. Create your content structure
4. Design your templates
5. Deploy to your favorite host

## Content Strategy

A great blog needs great content:

- Write regularly
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Optimize for SEO
- Engage with your readers
